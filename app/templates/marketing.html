Clarity without complexity

Ledger does one thing exceptionally well: give you a complete, accurate picture of your finances with the least possible friction.

Net Worth Timeline

Log every account once a month and Ledger builds a complete net worth history — investments, cash, property, liabilities. Charts show your trajectory at a glance with month-over-month and year-over-year deltas.

Monthly Snapshot Entry

A focused data-entry screen surfaces every account with its last balance pre-filled. Update only what changed. Track income and spending alongside balances, and let Ledger calculate your save rate automatically.

Path to Financial Independence

Model your retirement date with adjustable safe withdrawal rates, expected returns, and monthly contributions. Run multiple scenarios side by side and see exactly what levers move your FI date the most.

Dividend Income Modeling

AI fetches dividend yields for every holding you own and projects annual income — including DRIP reinvestment compounding. See how much passive income your portfolio generates today and will generate at retirement.

Monthly Update

In and out in five minutes, every time

The monthly update screen is designed for speed. Every account appears with its most recent balance pre-populated. You tab through, enter the numbers that changed, and you're done. Ledger handles the rest — net worth, save rate, month-over-month comparison.

  • Pre-filled balances from the previous month
  • Income & expense entry alongside balances
  • Automatic save rate and metrics calculation
  • Edit any historical month without breaking downstream data
Allocation Reports

Know exactly where your money is

Ledger automatically builds your asset allocation from your holdings and account categories. See your actual split across equities, bonds, real estate, and cash — and how far you are from your target allocation for each asset class.

  • Two-phase allocation: account-level and holding-level
  • Drift analysis versus your target percentages
  • AI-classified tickers mapped to asset classes automatically
  • Visual breakdown by account type and asset class
Projections

See your retirement date in real numbers

The Path to FI calculator uses your current portfolio, monthly contribution, and expected return to project your wealth forward. Adjust your safe withdrawal rate, add a mortgage payoff curve, and model multiple scenarios to find exactly what changes your timeline.

  • Monte Carlo–style scenario comparison
  • Safe withdrawal rate and SWR-based FI number
  • Mortgage amortization integrated into net worth projections
  • Visual timeline from today to financial independence
Powered by Claude

AI that does the tedious work for you

Ledger uses Claude to automatically classify every ticker in your portfolio into the right asset class — US equity, international, bonds, real estate, alternatives — so your allocation report is always accurate without manual tagging.

It also fetches dividend yields and models your projected annual income, with DRIP compounding, all without leaving the app.
